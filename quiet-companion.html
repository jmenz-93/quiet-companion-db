Getting started
How do I start a listening session?
Press the green microphone button in the left panel to begin recording. You'll see the waveform animate and the status badge change to "recording". Speak naturally — transcription happens in real time and appears below the mic.
What languages are supported?
Quiet Companion currently supports English (US), French, Spanish, Arabic, and Mandarin. More languages are added regularly. Use the language dropdown below the waveform to switch mid-session.
How does the output panel work?
After your voice query is processed, responses appear in the right panel as cards. Each card represents one result type — a text summary, a chart, a document attachment, or a clarifying question. You can scroll through results, copy individual cards, or export all results at once.
Tips for better results
Speak at a steady pace and avoid covering the microphone. Quiet Companion works best in low-noise environments.
Ask one focused question at a time. For complex queries, pause between parts to let the model process each step.
Say "attach" or "show me the document" to have the companion automatically pull relevant files into the output panel.

Troubleshooting
Microphone not detected
Check your browser's microphone permissions (usually in the address bar). Make sure no other application is using the mic exclusively. Refreshing the page often resolves this.
Transcription seems inaccurate
Ensure the correct language is selected. Try speaking closer to your microphone and reducing background noise. If accuracy is still low, switch to a wired headset for better capture quality.
Output cards not loading
Check your internet connection. If cards appear blank, try refreshing the session from the Sessions page. Large document attachments may take a few extra seconds to generate.
